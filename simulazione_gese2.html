About GESE Grade 2 Exam
GESE Grade 2 is the second level of Trinity College London's Graded Examinations in Spoken English. It corresponds to CEFR A1 level and takes approximately 6 minutes.

Language Functions at Grade 2:
  • Give personal information (name, age, where you live)
  • Talk about daily routines using present simple
  • Describe past events using past simple
  • Express likes, dislikes and preferences
  • Describe current activities using present continuous
  • Talk about future plans using "going to"
Key Grammar for Grade 2:
  • Present simple (I play, He plays)
  • Present continuous (I am playing)
  • Past simple regular/irregular verbs (I played, I went)
  • Going to future (I am going to play)
  • Can/Cannot for ability
  • Have got for possession
