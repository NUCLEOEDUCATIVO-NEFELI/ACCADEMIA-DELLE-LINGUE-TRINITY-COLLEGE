üéØ The Topic Task (Grade 4):

Before the exam, you prepare a topic of your choice and complete a Topic Form with 4 points. The examiner uses these points to discuss your topic with you.

Language Functions at Grade 4:
  • Describing people, places, and things using adjectives
  • Making comparisons (bigger than, more interesting than)
  • Talking about past events with dates/times
  • Talking about future plans and intentions
  • Expressing reasons and preferences (because)
  • Giving directions and instructions
Key Grammar for Grade 4:
  • Comparative and superlative adjectives (bigger, the biggest)
  • Adverbs of manner (quickly, carefully)
  • Past simple with when (When I was 5...)
  • Linking words (and, but, because, so)
  • Would like for requests/offers
  • Prepositions of place and movement
