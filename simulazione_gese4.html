  • Longer, more detailed Topic discussion with follow-up questions

Language Functions at Grade 4:
  • Describing past experiences in detail (Past continuous + simple)
  • Talking about hypothetical situations (If + past simple, would)
  • Expressing opinions and justifying them
  • Speculating about future possibilities
  • Describing processes and procedures
  • Making suggestions and recommendations
Key Grammar for Grade 4:
  • Past continuous vs Past simple (While I was walking, I saw...)
  • Second conditional (If I won the lottery, I would...)
  • Present perfect with ever/never (Have you ever...?)
  • Modals of deduction (must, might, can't)
  • Passive voice (simple forms)
  • Reported speech (said that, told me)
The Topic Task - Preparation
Your Topic: "An Unforgettable Journey I Took"
