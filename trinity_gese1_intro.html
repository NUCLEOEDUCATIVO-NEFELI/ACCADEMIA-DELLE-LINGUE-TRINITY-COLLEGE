About GESE Grade 1 Exam
Exam Duration: 5 minutes
CEFR Level: Pre-A1 (Initial Stage)
Format: One-to-one conversation with Trinity examiner

Language Functions

  • Exchange greetings with the examiner
  • Give personal information (name, age)
  • Identify and name items from the vocabulary list
  • Leave-taking (say goodbye)

What the Examiner Will Ask

Example questions:
‚Ä¢ "What's your name?"
‚Ä¢ "How old are you?"
‚Ä¢ "What colour is this?"
‚Ä¢ "How many [objects]?"
‚Ä¢ "What's this?" (pointing to object/picture)
Exam Tips for Grade 1
  • Speak clearly and loudly
  • Use short, simple answers
  • Listen carefully to the examiner's instructions
  • Don't worry about mistakes - keep talking!
  • Practice saying numbers 1-20 fluently
  • Know your colours and basic objects
