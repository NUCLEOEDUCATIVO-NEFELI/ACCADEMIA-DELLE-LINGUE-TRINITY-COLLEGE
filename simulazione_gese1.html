About GESE Grade 1 Exam
GESE Grade 1 is the first level of Trinity College London's Graded Examinations in Spoken English. It corresponds to CEFR Pre-A1 level and takes approximately 5 minutes.

What You Need to Know:
  • Greetings: Hello, Good morning, Goodbye
  • Personal information: name, age
  • Numbers 1-20
  • Colours: red, blue, green, yellow, etc.
  • Common objects: book, pen, bag, chair, etc.
  • Animals: cat, dog, bird, etc.
