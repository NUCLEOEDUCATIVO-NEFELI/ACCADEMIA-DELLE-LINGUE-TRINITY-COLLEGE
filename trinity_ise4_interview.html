üìù Template C2 per Topic Presentation:

ü§ù Task 3: Interactive Task
5 minuti Collaborative
Requisiti C2: Task collaborativo con l'esaminatore (role-play, negotiation, problem-solving). Devi manage the interaction, negotiate meaning e achieve outcomes mantenendo fluency sofisticata.

Scenario di esempio: Policy Negotiation

Scenario: You are an advisor to the Ministry of Education. Your colleague (the examiner) advocates for mandatory AI literacy courses from primary school. You have concerns about implementation and equity. Negotiate a policy framework that addresses both perspectives.

Obiettivo: Raggiungere un accordo condiviso in 5 minuti, dimostrando:
  • Active listening e paraphrasing
  • Proposal di alternative
  • Compromise e consensus-building
  • Managing disagreement diplomatically

üí° Linguaggio C2 per Interactive Task:

  • Paraphrasing: "If I understand you correctly, you're suggesting..." / "So your position is..."
  • Proposing alternatives: "Might we consider...?" / "What if we were to...?"
  • Hedging disagreement: "I see merit in that, though I wonder whether..." / "That's certainly one approach. An alternative might be..."
  • Building consensus: "It seems we agree on X. Where we differ is Y. Could we...?"
  • Closing: "So to summarize what we've agreed..." / "Shall we settle on...?"
