About GESE Grade 5 Exam

üé≠ The Interactive Task (NEW in Grade 5):

The examiner presents a situation or problem. You must:

  • Ask questions to find out more information
  • Make suggestions and proposals
  • Respond to the examiner's comments
  • Negotiate and reach a conclusion together

This is NOT a monologue - you must interact!

Language Functions at Grade 5:
  • Expressing and responding to feelings and attitudes
  • Expressing opinions and making assumptions
  • Speculating about past, present and future events
  • Managing discourse (interrupting, changing topic, returning to topic)
  • Making, accepting and rejecting suggestions
  • Narrating events with detail and evaluation
Key Grammar for Grade 5:
  • Third conditional (If I had known, I would have...)
  • Mixed conditionals (If I had studied, I would be...)
  • Modals in the past (must have, can't have, could have)
  • Wish/If only (I wish I had...)
  • Phrasal verbs and idiomatic expressions
  • Discourse markers (On the other hand, Having said that, Anyway)

The Topic Task - Preparation
Your Topic: "A Difficult Decision I Had to Make"

In Grade 5, your topic should allow for discussion of emotions, speculation about alternatives, and evaluation of outcomes.

üìù Topic Form

Complete these discussion points:

Point 1: What the situation was and what options I had
Point 2: Why the decision was difficult and my feelings at the time
Point 3: What I decided and how I reached that decision
Point 4: What happened afterwards and how I feel about it now
üí° Grade 5 Tip: Be ready to discuss what might have happened if you had chosen differently. The examiner will ask speculative questions using third conditional!
